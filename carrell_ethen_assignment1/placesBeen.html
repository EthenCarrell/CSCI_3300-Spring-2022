Places I have been

There are only a few notable places that I have been. I have been to both New York City, New York and San Francisco, California. When I was in my junior year of high school, my school band traveled to New York City to participate in the Saint Patrick's day parade that is held there yearly. It was a long 3 mile march when you are a tuba player, but I had a fantastic time! The buildings were so large and liked nothing I had ever seen before. The amount of people was also hard to get accustomed to. There was always something to do and the sound never stopped. Getting to the top of the Empire State building was probably my favorite part. Seeing everything was so high up was frightening and amazing!

I went to San Francisco because I was one of the finalist in the Dev Catalyst competition in the State of Tennessee. I had made a website that ended up getting me a spot in the prize pool group. The prize was getting to see major companies like Google and Facebook in Silicon Valley. This was one of the coolest experiences of my life. Getting to see the west coast for the first time was amazing and all the tech companies blew my mind. Seeing professionals work in a large company was eye opening to say the least. The Golden Gate Bridge was one of my favorite parts. It is a lot smaller than the movies make it out to be. However, it was still cool to see in person.

I enjoyed my travels and got to see a lot of new things that I would never see in my hometown. I am hoping to travel more in the future -- maybe even to another continent!
