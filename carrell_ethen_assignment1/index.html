School Life

My school life is pretty busy year round. I work in Housing at Austin Peay State Univeristy as a Residential Housing Director over the building Governor's Terrace North. I love my job because I get to work with so many wonderful people, but there are some long days and nights at times. I am pursing a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Theory and System. My degree is challenging, but it is worth the hardships it brings. I enjoy finding solutions to problems, so I could not think of a better degree to fit me. I have some school friends I like to hangout with when I get a chance. Going out to eat is really what I do the most with my friends. Also, I am a scholarship worker for the computer science department. This also requires more of my time each week, but I do feel like it helps me improve as a student and programmer each semester.
